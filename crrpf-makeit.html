Title: Make It
Fandom: Critical Role RPF
Summary: Taliesin decides not to be patient zero
Word count: 700

Make It

It really seemed like nothing. Taliesin woke with a scratchy throat and spent the day nursing it with honeyed tea. He wasn’t too worried, he honestly just wanted an excuse to drink more tea, as it was the only thing that kept his throat soothed. He developed a small, tickly cough later in the day along with having to regularly clear his throat. No big deal, could be the weather. Still, he caught Matt giving him the Mom Friend look.

"What is it?"

Matt looked dismissive for a second, he wanted to pretend he wasn’t staring. "Are you good?"

Taliesin wasn’t sure what he meant until Matt gestured toward the fresh cup of tea sitting in front of him. "Yeah, yeah, throat's just a little sore."

"Hope you're not coming down with anything after con."

"Naw, I'm fine."

Matt believed him. Taliesin believed himself.

And then he woke the next morning.

The first thing he did was start hot water for tea. The first cough of the day scraped against his stinging throat, causing him to scrunch his face in pain. He no longer wanted to swallow anything, but he still took the tea with him back to his room.

It felt like liquid was dripping down his upper lip, and it was confirmed when he rubbed at his nose and his knuckles came away glistening with moisture. Sniffling proved to be the wrong decision when he felt a pinprick in his nose.

Taliesin rushed to put his tea down and steepled his hands over his mouth and nose just in time to catch the sneeze. The stinging in his throat intensified for a miserable few seconds. Taliesin stifled a whimper.

It was funny, in hindsight, that his first concern was "what about the game session tonight?" because his entire body had turned against him and he chose to worry about playing D&D with his friends. It was just a cold; he'd be fine by tonight.

-----

"I can still make it," Taliesin insisted. He sat on his bed, doing some idle scrolling on his laptop as he talked to Matt. He hadn't felt like doing much else today.

"Buddy, you sound horrible."

Taliesin scoffed, rubbed at his nose and pretended that wasn’t the worst thing a voice actor could hear. "I know, but…"

"Stay home, get some rest."

"I've been resting all day. I'll be fine for a few hours, won't I? Seriously, it's not a problem."

"Come on, don't be patient zero," Matt said.

Taliesin was going to reply before he felt a familiar tickle in his throat. He pulled the phone away to muffle a cough into his arm. He recovered with a sniffle, trying to ignore the cold liquid he could feel at the base of his nostrils.

"Okay..." He sighed.

He fell into a misery-induced sleep not too long after the call and woke just in time to turn on twitch. The sounds of his roommates pattering around and making dinner outside the door was comforting after being home alone all day.

"Welcome to Critical Role!"

Taliesin smiled at the intro. It was strange seeing only Laura and Sam on their side of the table.

"Sadly no Taliesin tonight! He's very sick." Taliesin didn’t remember using the word 'very'. Apparently Matt knew he was lying about feeling well enough to go out. Of course he did. "So he's home, probably watching- hopefully watching this so you can keep up with... How I ruin your character while you're gone." Oh God that's right, Caduceus was going to suffer. "Get better, buddy."

Taliesin smiled. And then rolled his eyes as everyone cracked jokes at his expense. They'd regret it in a week, he was sure.

"Fucking Liam..." He muttered fondly.

"So, feel better Taliesin," and his heart warmed.

It was hard to focus on what was happening when his body was attacking him. Every few minutes was a cough or a sneeze, and he had to get up and search the house for some kind of medicine at some point.

One of his roommates only had Tylenol to offer. It wasn't heavy cold meds, but it would work. He gratefully accepted it with a million weary 'thank you's and retreated into his room once again as to not spread germs throughout the whole house.

At some point he caught Matt playing Caduceus and couldn't help but cringe, and then laugh.

He let himself pass out before the stream ended. Hopefully Matt would forgive him, but he couldn’t stay awake another second.
