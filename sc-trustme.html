Title: Trust Me
Fandom: Schitt's Creek
Summary: Patrick offers some post-panic attack comfort
Word count: 200

Trust Me

Patrick descended the stairs to find his husband on the couch. “Hey.”

David looked up. He looked exhausted, despite having been doing nothing but resting all day.

“Hey,” he said, voice rough from disuse. He offered a smile that didn’t quite reach his eyes, and hummed happily as Patrick sat next to him and let him lay his head on his shoulder.

Patrick began carding his fingers through David’s hair. “How’re you feeling?”

“Slightly less...” David made a thoughtful sound. In the end, he just shrugged lazily. “Slightly less, I think.”

He nodded, pressing a kiss to David’s temple and then continuing to run his hand through his hair. “I’m glad.”

David huffed out a sigh. “I’m sorry about today,” he muttered quietly. “That was a lot... for you, I mean.”

“Don’t apologize. I’ll be fine.”

“Thank you.” David was still a little out of it. Even though he was trying to hide it, to act normal, Patrick could tell. That panic attack took a lot out of him. “I’m sorry,” he breathed out again.

“David,” Patrick kissed his head again. “I love you so much,” another kiss. “And I will always be here. Don’t apologize.”

David nodded against him. “You’re amazing.”

“Oh, trust me, I know.”

David laughed, the first genuine laugh he’d heard from him all day. “Good.”
