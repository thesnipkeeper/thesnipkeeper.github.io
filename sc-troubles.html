Title: Troubles
Fandom: Schitt's Creek
Summary: David's having some stomach issues, Patrick sends him home
Word count: 300

Troubles

His stomach hurts. A lot. David has no idea what he’s eaten that caused such turmoil in his digestive system, but if it could kindly stop before he embarrasses himself, he’d appreciate it.

He’s been fuzzy all day, distracted by his stomach and the weird headache that’s forming. Not to mention he’s just tired. He feels like death.

And he certainly wants to die when he sees his business partner and... whatever else Patrick is, giving him a strange look as he excuses himself to the bathroom for the third time in an hour.

Patrick offers one of his patented white guy smiles as David returns a few minutes later.

“Everything okay?” he asks, innocently as ever.

“Yeah, why?” David replies... innocently as ever. If there’s anything he’s skilled at, it’s pretending that nothing is wrong.

Patrick shrugs. “You’ve just uh,” he clears his throat. “Been going to the bathroom a lot today.”

David wants to hide for eternity. Or at least a year. He’s done it before, he can do it again. He can just... change his name, move under a bridge... anything would be better than ruining a new relationship by admitting to stomach troubles.

Patrick must sense his distress, because he chuckles and squeezes his shoulder. “David, I wasn’t trying to make you uncomfortable. I’m sorry. You’ve just been acting kind of like you’re not feeling good and I’m worried.”

David sighs. “I’m not. My stomach...” he gestures toward his general abdominal region. “Is unhappy about something that I had for breakfast.”

Patrick has another strange look before he winces and says, “You sure it was breakfast? There’s that stomach virus going around, y’know...”

Oh God. David hadn’t even thought about that. He swallows and closes his eyes, tilting his head back. “Fuuuuuck.”

Patrick laughs. “Why don’t you go home and rest?”

David nods, sighing. “Call me if you need help, though?”

“I’ll be fine. Go.”
