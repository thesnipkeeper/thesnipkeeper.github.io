Movie Night

He was fine when they dropped him off that night. It'd been a later drive than they would've liked, running into traffic and terrible weather.

Taliesin had fallen asleep in the backseat after a long day of convention events. They were all exhausted and more than ready to go home.

He thanked them for the ride, told them he'd see them tomorrow for the movie night Matt and Marisha were hosting - a fun little get-together to help them wind down after a stressful weekend.

"Get some rest, okay?" Matt said as Taliesin pulled his luggage out of the car. He looked about as beat as they all did.

Taliesin smiled tiredly, throwing his duffle over his shoulder.

"Try and stop me."

They watched him walk into the dark house - his roommates probably long asleep by now - and went home to sleep off the convention fatigue.

-----

Travis and Laura arrived, soon followed by Sam and Liam. They brought various snacks, a couple of pizzas, and some soda that was sure to keep them all awake during the movie. Everyone was still tired, but happy to be there and spending time with just their friends and no one else.

Liam was snacking away in the kitchen. "We starting soon? I wanna make a run to the store and grab some chip dip."

"Do we really need dip?" Laura asked.

"I do."

"Anyone heard from Taliesin?" Marisha checked her phone. Her eyebrows knitted together as she scrolled for any missed texts.

There was a chorus of 'nope, not me's in the room. She was ready to call him when there was a knock at the door.

"I'll get it," Matt rushed over and opened the door with a flourish. There was Taliesin looking just as tired as the rest of them.

And also a bit red in the face?

"So sorry I'm late," Taliesin said as he walked in. His voice was thick and deep, and he gave a stuffy snuffle while Matt closed the door behind him. "I'm not even going to lie, my nap went longer than expected."

"That's okay! We're just eating... Are you coming down with something?"

Taliesin smiled a bit sheepishly, chuckling and running a hand through his hair. His nose and eyes were both red. Matt had been friends with the man long enough to know what he looked and sounded like when he had a cold.

"I uh- yeah, I think so. I woke up with it."

Matt made a sympathetic noise. "We always think we've escaped the con plague."

"I know it," Taliesin agreed. "I'll make sure to keep my germs to myself tonight."

"I'm not too worried." He laid a hand on Taliesin's back and led him into the living room with everyone else. Taliesin's shirt felt a bit clammy, but it was to be expected with LA weather.

Taliesin's symptoms scattered throughout the first movie. Everyone could hear his nose getting stuffier and more of a bother. Eventually he grabbed the tissue box from the bathroom, and set it next to him. He never bothered to blow his nose, choosing instead to simply rub at his nostrils as if that would do any good.

"Sorry, sorry..." He whispered after a couple of stuffy sneezes. The others glanced but didn't really pay any mind.

Matt caught Marisha giving him the same look he himself was giving Taliesin.

The poor guy sounded miserable. A collection of used tissues was building in his lap, and he was beginning to breathe through his mouth, giving the occasional sniffle as if to test his congestion.

The first movie ended, Taliesin disappeared, and Matt approached his ailing friend as everyone disbanded to get more snacks.

Taliesin hadn't bothered to shut the door all the way when he'd escaped into the far bathroom, and Matt could hear the sounds of horribly congested nose blowing as he got closer. He nudged the door open a bit and leaned against the frame.

Taliesin looked up over a wad of tissues. One eyebrow raised.

"Do you mind?" He said without any real venom. They were past privacy, really, but Matt was aware he was being weird. When Matt didn't answer immediately, Taliesin blew his nose into the tissues once more and then rubbed the excess liquid from his nostrils.

"Want some Nyquil or something?" Matt asked with all the sympathy in the world. "Marisha and I have a good collection from that cold we passed around a while ago."

Taliesin sniffled thoroughly, seeming to finally be able to get some air through his nose. "No, that's alright," his voice caught and he cleared his throat. "I'm just... I might head home. I don't know why I came in the first place, I guess I just wanted to spend some time with everyone. I just depleted your tissue supply instead," he added with a chuckle.

Matt couldn't help but pout a bit at the thought of Taliesin leaving. "Well, you can deplete our supply as much as you'd like if you want to stay."

Taliesin looked like he was about to reply, before he got a look of dread, taking a deep breath and pulled his arm up to cover his mouth. He gave a hard cough that sounded so painful it had Matt wincing just as hard.

"We have cough drops and lozenges too..." Matt said with a sympathetic smile.

Taliesin sighed and took a few seconds to think. "Sorry, I'm sorry. I really don't feel well, and I'm not much fun right now, and I don't want to get anyone else sick..."

Matt was waiting for the "and I want to go home" but it never came. His brows knitted together in concern as Taliesin shuddered.

"Chills?" Matt asked worriedly

"I might also be running a fever," Taliesin said with annoyance.

Matt lifted his hand to Taliesin's head, the other leaning into it. It wasn't their first 'how sick are you' rodeo.

"Yeah, you're a little warm."

Taliesin groaned.

"Do you want to go home?"

"Not especially," he admitted. "About those meds?"

"I've gotcha." Matt led him into his and Marisha's bathroom, digging through various drawers until he finally found the blessed cold meds that made their lives bearable when the two of them were sick.

He read the back of the label as Taliesin had seated himself on the edge of the tub, sniffling at a newly-runny nose. He looked so exhausted, Matt wondered if he really should go home. But he didn't seem to want to.

"It might make you tired," Matt admitted. "But you'll feel better."

"You're an angel, truly," Taliesin told him as he accepted the measured cup.

"Hey, are you guys coming back pretty soon?" Marisha called as she walked in. She frowned the moment she saw him knock back the Nyquil. "Aw, Tal..."

"I'm good," Taliesin sniffled, handing the cup back. "I mean, I will be good."

-----

They started the second movie. Everyone was beginning to wind down and get sleepy. Matt and Marisha were on each side of Taliesin, passing him tissues and cooing over him no matter how embarrassed or Done he acted. He started to shiver a little more as the movie went on. Matt grabbed the throw off of the back of the couch.

"I'm fine!" Taliesin protested with a sniffle.

Laura wacked both of them. "Shh! This is the best part."

Matt didn’t listen to any argument, and the shivering subsided a bit once Taliesin was covered up. His head fell on Marisha's shoulder as she wrapped an arm around him.

He sniffled pitifully for most of the movie, but some rough coughs were added to the mix.

By the time the movie ended, Taliesin was snoring.
