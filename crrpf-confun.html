Matt exhales noisily through his mouth. He looks into the plastic bag in his hands. Medicine, tissues, that gross ramen Tal likes, and some random things that might help. He knocks on the door.

Seconds go by, and then the sound of quiet shuffling. Matt waits patiently before the handle of the door turns and opens. Taliesin peaks out, and his eyes widen before he opens the door the rest of the way. One corner of his mouth lifts into a sleepy smile.

He looks awful, if Matt is being honest. His hair is a complete change from its usual styling, messy and unkempt. He's in plaid pajama bottoms and a stretched t-shirt that's probably at least ten years old. His eyes are red-rimmed and puffy, carrying exhausted bags like he hasn't slept in days, and his nose is beginning to redden around the nostrils and above his upper lip.

"I told you not to come," he says, managing to sound disappointed but at the same time look so relieved to see someone.

"Well, you also told us that you weren't that sick and, standing in front of you right now, I know that's not true."

Taliesin looks like he's going to argue (or maybe apologize) before his breath catches in his throat and he has to scrambled to cover a cough into his shirt collar. Matt catches a pained wince flicker across his face and then the coughing dissipates. He clears his throat, a painful, scratchy sound that has Matt wincing as well.

"Mind if I come in?"

Taliesin sniffles, and then gives a chuckle despite himself. "Don’t say I didn’t warn you!"

The curtains in the room are still drawn, the TV casting a gentle glow over the room. By the state of the bed, it's obvious that Taliesin hasn’t really left it until Matt showed up. Taliesin, ever the wonderful host, stays standing instead of crawling back into the warm bed like he ought to.

"Have a seat, my friend! I bring gifts."

"Oh God, what'd you do?" his voice has a certain thickness that tells

Matt seats himself on the edge of the bed as he rummages through the bag. "Seriously! Sit down, you're the sick one here."

So Taliesin does, keeping some distance between the two of them as he paws at his nose.

Matt presents the medicine and extra-soft tissues, and makes quick use of them when Taliesin has to turn away to sneeze. He accepts the tissue with a nod of thanks and blows his nose. His lips stay parted, a testament to how congested he is.

"You're going to get sick if you stay here any longer, you know," he mutters, still rubbing angrily at his pinkened nostrils.

Matt's already opening the medicine and pouring a dose. "I'll be fine," he assures. "I'm more worried about you right now."

"Don’t be," he's looking utterly miserable as he says it, sniffling at his congested sinuses and then wincing when it seems to worsen a headache. "I should just be glad that it happened here and not when I'm supposed to be recording."

Ah yes, getting a cold, a voice actor's truest nightmare.

Matt nods and hands him the little cup. Taliesin knocks it back without question.

"You look like you could use a nap."

Taliesin gives a quick sniff and rubs at his eyes. He nods. "Yeah… yeah. I don’t know why I'm so tired, I fell asleep super early last night."

He makes a sympathetic noise in the back of his throat. "Well, you're sick, buddy. Your body needs a little more rest than usual," Matt rubs his back and presses into his side. He feels a little warm, a little sweaty, Matt starts to suspect a fever. Hopefully the medicine will help with that. He brushes his hand against Taliesin's neck, receiving no reaction in return, and just as he suspected, Taliesin's skin is warmer than usual.

His friend makes a noise that says something along the lines of "yeah, I know, but it is what it is".

Matt stands up, leaving the bag on the bed. "Shit, I've gotta go. Our panel is in like ten minutes." he leans down and gives Taliesin a quick side-hug. "Get some rest, alright? Someone will be back to check on you later."

"No no no! Seriously, Matt, I don’t want anyone else getting sick."

"Okay… then do you want us to leave you alone all day? Let you have some time to yourself to sleep this off?"

There's a moment of silence as Taliesin contemplates. He sniffles miserably, running the back of his hand under his nose and then grabbing a tissue when he realizes his hand isn't going to cut it. He looks up at Matt as he rubs his nose through the tissue, sighing heavily.

"No…" he admits, pulling himself back into bed, but not covering up.

Matt nods. "Alright. See you later?"

"Y-yeah..." Taliesin clears his throat, and winces. "Wait, take my room key."

"You sure?"

"I'm not going anywhere."

Matt smiles and makes a move to leave before he hesitates. He turns around, rushes to the bed, and presses a light kiss to his friend's forehead.

Taliesin makes a soft noise, mouth quirking up in a sleepy smile. "Have fun."

He wakes to soft voices and the hotel mattress dipping under someone's weight. No one speaks to him or tries to wake him, but Taliesin decides to open his eyes.

He catches Marisha's attention with a groan. "Wow, welcome to the land of the living, huh? Sorry, we were trying to be quiet."

He rubs the sleep out of his eyes, chuckling at the sight before him. Matt is on the bed next to him, leaning against the headboard. Marisha is cross-legged at the end. The TV is playing food network, definitely not what Taliesin had it turned to earlier. As a rule, Taliesin's hotel room has always been the one that the group hangs out in.

"It's-" His voice breaks, his throat feeling completely raw. If sleep is supposed to be good for you when you're sick, then why does he feel so much worse? "It's fine. I was waiting for you guys." he finishes with a cough that scrapes at his already-sore throat.

Matt looks over with an expression painted with worry.

"You sound like shit," Marisha says, attention going back to the TV.

Taliesin nods. "I feel like shit."

He wants to sit up and join them in the TV-watching, but when Taliesin tries to move, his muscles cry out in protest and his body decides that despite the nap, it's even more exhausted now. No energy. So he stays lying down, trying to ignore the aches and pain and discomfort completely engulfing his body from the inside out.

So this isn’t just a cold, then. His memory goes back to a news broadcast that mentioned a flu outbreak in LA. Fuck.

Taliesin feels an itch in his throat and doesn't have the energy to do anything but turn into his pillow and try to muffle it. The position coupled with the harsh cough makes his stomach feel unsettled.

He shudders and huddles further under the duvet. Every bit of exposed skin feels like it's coated in ice. "It's freezing in here, oh my God." He mutters without bothering to open his mouth too much.

Matt's expression gets more worried. He looks like he's going to say something, but Marisha beats him to it.

"Dude, it's like 75 in here."

Taliesin blinks. Right. Fever... Chills... His body faking him out. He feels a hand on his forehead and Matt makes a thoughtful noise.

A sigh, "Think I've got the flu," he mutters honestly.

Matt frowns out of sympathy.

With the flu comes a new level of concern. Marisha and Matt are the most important parts of their cast, if they go down everything else will too. "Don’t want you two to get sick."

"Hey, don’t worry about that, okay? We do wash our hands and shit sometimes." Marisha jokes.

It's going to be a long weekend.

Taliesin hears a knock at his door after the first panel of the day is supposed to be over. It opens to Matt and Marisha. Matt's in his usual outfit, but upon further inspection, he looks exhausted and his cheeks are flushed like he's overheated.

"Hey buddy," Matt smiles sheepishly as they make their way into the room, before coughing into the crook of his arm.

"Fuck."

Marisha nods and sighs. "He woke up feeling pretty shitty."

"I'm sorry..." Taliesin looks as apologetic as ever, but Matt shakes his head.

"Not your fault. You warned us," he chuckles.

Marisha snorted. "Safety. Text us if you need anything, okay?"

She gives them each a kiss on the forehead before making her way out, and then it's just the two of them.

Taliesin sighs at their current shitty situation. He pulls the blankets back and pats the bed.

"Come on, get comfortable."

Matt doesn't hesitate. The two of them end up cuddling pretty comfortably after he gets out of his usual clothes.

"How'd you make it through the panel?"

Matt groans. "A ton of drugs. I didn't wanna miss this one so Marisha bought a ton of stuff to help me survive for a couple hours." He sniffled. "They've worn off now, though."

The two of them fall asleep together.

Taliesin is sweating as he's been doing off and on, but Matt seems to still be shivering. He reaches and grabs from the absolute pharmacy that Matt brought the day before and measures out a dose. If Matt refuses it, he'll just take it himself.

Matt's eyebrows furrow and Taliesin tries to nudge him awake. It's not that hard to do, which tells Taliesin he was hardly sleeping anyway.

"Hey buddy, why don’t you take something? It might help you rest easier, you know?" He says with a yawn.

Matt groans, more at his body than at Taliesin, and manages to sit up. He looks exactly how Taliesin feels, like he wants to curse his own body for falling for this nasty bug. The minute Matt gets settled against the headboard and starts to reach for the medicine, however, he starts scrubbing at his head and eyes.

"Dizzy?"

Matt nods. He sniffles, which makes him cough, long and worryingly hard. And again. Suddenly, his eyes go wide and he's bolting to the bathroom.

He doesn’t have time to close the door, so Taliesin can hear all the nauseating sounds coming from the bathroom. The nice thing about hotel rooms is that everything is close. He doesn’t have to worry about getting Matt back to bed, because he'll make it eventually.

Taliesin sighs and downs the medicine himself.

The last time they were sick together, it was at Matt and Marisha's house when Marisha was at work. Taliesin feels like, as a forty year-old man, that was the one time he learned how to truly fend for himself, he thinks fondly.

Matt gives a particularly harsh heave that goes breathless at the end.

"Matt?" Taliesin calls. His voice isn't very strong at the moment. "Are you gonna be okay? Do you want me to come in there?"

A few seconds. "Yeah, I'm fine. Give me a sec…"

Taliesin waits, fidgeting, until Matt emerges from the bathroom. His face is red, he looks miserable, but he chuckles and flops back in bed.

"We're a mess," Matt slurs sleepily.

Taliesin scoffs. "So we are."
