Grump

"Oh my God. Come on."

The others flinch, but Sam just sighs. It's been going on for a while.

"What's up with Liam today?" Marisha whispers.

"Don't ask me. He's been in a mood all day."

"Fuck." turns all of their heads to Liam.

"How's it going, Liam?" Sam calls over to his friend, who's smacking his computer like a mad man.

"Great. It's going great," he answers with a deep sigh.

Sam turns to Marisha and Taliesin and gestures with his hand as if 'see?'.

"Liam, you okay?"

Liam sighs, obviously having been asked that several times today. He runs a hand through his hair and closes his laptop. "Yeah, sorry. Just tired." They realize that yeah, he definitely looks exhausted.

"Rough night?" Marisha offers as she sits down to her own computer.

Liam yawns before adding, "Well, I was cleaning up puke all night. So that was fantastic. I don't know how kids have so much in them."

Sam winces and nods. "I hear ya. Kids good?"

Liam nods. "Yeah, they're fine now. They bounce back pretty fast. Me however..." He groans. "I could use a nap."

Sam can see it a mile away. He and Liam have been friends long enough that he can tell when his friend is coming down with something, and it's pretty much to be expected if his family's been sick. Liam stops acknowledging anyone else, getting very quiet and focused.

Without a word, he stands and heads to the bathroom, and Sam knows exactly why. He sits anxiously.

-----

Matt shows up at the studio from a VO just as Liam is stumbling into the bathroom. He looks over to Taliesin in the lobby, who looks up from his computer with a sigh.

"Is he… okay?"

"Stomach bug," Taliesin says simply.

Matt tries not to sigh, but a sarcastic, "fantastic," comes out anyway. When one of them gets sick, it's more than likely many others will too. Still, he can't help but feel sympathetic when he hears Liam gagging in the bathroom.

Taliesin seems to catch his drift and nods in agreement. "Yeah, I don’t know why he bothered showing up." He's trying to be funny but his face is riddled with concern. Matt wonders how long this has been going on before he showed up. Taliesin seems to read his face. "He'll be fine, I'm sure. I mean, he probably feels really shitty right now, but…"

There's the sound of whimpering from the bathroom, and suddenly Matt is rushing over without a second thought. Shit. Shit shit shit. He's reminded how much Liam hates throwing up. If there's one thing that can break him, it's nausea.

He thinks back to a few years ago, a little get-together in Taliesin's hotel room at con to celebrate getting through a particularly busy weekend in one piece. They all got shitfaced. Liam ended up spending the night in the bathroom, crying until he fell asleep on the tile floor.

He knocks, sparing a glance at Taliesin in the lobby who is looking just as alert and concerned.

"Occupied."

"It's Matt. Can I come in and check on you?"

A broken, "Think 's open."

Liam has an arm draped over the toilet seat, limply resting his head over it. He lifts it just a little when Matt closes the door behind him, with red, watery eyes. He sniffles, resting his head back down while facing his friend.

"Oh, buddy..." Matt coos.

Liam smiles, nose running and eyes puffy. There's a shiny streak of saliva on his chin he hasn't bothered to wipe away.

"Welcome to the party," Liam chuckles despite himself. Matt has a feeling his tears aren't just from throwing up. He's been crying. "What do you want?"

Matt frowns. "Just making sure you're alright. Taliesin said you have a bug?"

Liam nods, running the back of his hand against his nose before reaching up to flush the toilet. "Kids were sick last night," his voice is so roughed up Matt feels guilty for making him talk.

He walks to the sink and wets a paper towel before kneeling down in front of his sick friend, wiping the sweat and saliva away without any protest from Liam.

"So I know that this might come as a shock to you, but I think you should go home," he says, pulling damp pieces of hair away from where they're plastered on his forehead.

Liam huffs out a laugh. "Amy dropped me off this morning. Car's in the shop, remember? Anyway, I don't..." He swallows. "I don't think I can ride in a vehicle right now. Or like, move much at all," the end breaks up, tears building in his eyes.

Matt makes a sympathetic noise and reaches up to rub his arm. "Hey, it's alright. You can just get comfortable here for a while, hm?"

Liam doesn't look too sure. Actually, he looks like he's going to call Matt crazy for letting some infectious being stay in the studio all day. But his stomach interrupts anything he was going to say, and suddenly he's lunging for the toilet.

Matt rubs his back as he heaves, somehow having something left in him to vomit up.

The moment Liam's bout comes to an end, he's sobbing. He buries his head in his hands as if it'll save his dignity.

"Oh no no no, shh, come here," Matt pulls him in, letting Liam cry into his shoulder and hoping he remembers to change his shirt before the next stream. He wraps his arms around the shuddering frame, rubbing his back and offering comforting words.

They relax into each other, or as relaxed as one can be on the floor of a bathroom, and stay like that for a while. There's unintelligible conversation going on outside.

Knock knock knock

The both of them jump.

"I'm coming in, yell now if you're not wearing pants."

Matt laughs. Marisha.

Marisha enters without much apology, takes in the situation, and sighs. "Taliesin said you weren't having a good time in here. Brought you a bottle of water." She sets it on the floor next to Liam, who's sitting up off of Matt. "To like, rinse your mouth. You should probably let your stomach chill for a while before you drink anything."

"Taliesin is a fucking loud mouth," Liam says with a scoff. "Love that guy."

-----

Sam scrolls through his phone with one hand and pets Liam's head with the other. He isn't being particularly cuddly, more just passively lying on Sam while he waits for his body to rebel against him even more. His stomach is making noises Sam pretends not to hear.

"For the love of God, please don’t puke on me."

Liam groans and shifts. "No promises." his stomach groans as well.

They sit comfortably silent before Liam's breath begins to even out. He's asleep. Taliesin and Marisha join the quiet area, if only to enjoy the fact that everyone's trying to keep the place calm for Liam's sake.

Eventually, Taliesin ends up leaning on Sam's shoulder as he scrolls idly through his phone, and the Marisha against Taliesin.

Just a little while before Talks.

With Liam and Sam.

Fuck, they need to figure that out. Can Liam do Talks like this? Maybe he's feeling better.

With almost comedic timing, Liam squirms and barely leans over the edge, before puking. It narrowly misses Sam's leg, but his shoes and the carpet aren't so lucky.

Marisha and Taliesin jump.

"Ohhh fuck, Liam," Marisha says.

They manage to get him to the bathroom without much incident, Liam still out of it from his rude awakening. They make an attempt to clean up. Sam's shoe will never be the same.

Talks will be fun.

-----

"Did you two have a good day?" Brian asks, sitting back and crossing his legs. "Liam, I heard you weren't feeling so great."

Liam chuckles and shrugs. He looks a lot better now that they plastered makeup on him. "Yeah no, I've been puking literally all day. It's been great."

"Shit man, hope you feel better."

"Yeah! Me too."

Surprisingly, Talks goes really well. While Liam isn't as lively as usual, he's just as cuddly. Sam can feel a budding fever radiating off of his friend, who's cheek is pressed against his shoulder while he answers Brian's question about Nott.

They end their questioning with light conversation.

"That's all we have for tonight," Says Brian. "Go get some rest, Liam. You look fucking wrecked."

-----

Liam had texted Matt Wednesday evening, promising that he was feeling much better and that he would be there for Critical Role.

Matt goes to sleep early that night, having spent most of the day out and about.

He wakes at 4AM with a cramping stomach. He squirms, trying to go back to sleep and get a couple hours more rest before another big day ahead. 4:30, after a time of struggling to get comfortable, the ache in his stomach worsens and begins to overflow into nausea. Matt sits up, swinging his legs over the edge of the bed, being careful not to wake his wife, and pads toward the bathroom.

He waits a miserable few minutes before stomach contents force their way up his throat. He gags, and heaves, trying to get it over with as quickly as possible. Nope. This is going to be one of those nights where he can't leave the bathroom, huh? Matt sighs in defeat and sits on the floor, awaiting the next bout of puking. He shivers and looks around for something to warm him up.

-----

Marisha takes in the sight before her. Her husband is curled up on the tile floor, head resting on his arm, towel wrapped lamely around his shoulders. When Matt wasn’t in bed when she woke up, she had a feeling. It sucks to be right.

"Shit."

Matt's eyes flutter open. He groans, realizing where he is and how awful he feels.

"Might've picked up Liam's flu," he says hoarsely.

Marisha chuckles. She kneels down beside him and brushes the hair out of his face.

"You think?"
