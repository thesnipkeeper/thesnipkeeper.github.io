Anytime

Molly shudders. He’s been curled up in bed since they returned to the inn from their job, listening to everyone else drink and have fun with bitter jealousy. Damn it, he should be out there drunkenly singing with Jester and Veth.

The door to his and Fjord’s room opens with a creek, though his back is to it, he knows exactly who it is by the lazy gait and heavy footsteps that approach. Caduceus takes his time walking around the room, probably observing and putting everything together, before finally getting in Molly’s line of sight.

Molly looks up rather pitifully.

“You aren’t looking so hot.”

“That sounds about right,” Mollymauk grinds out through chattering teeth. He whimpers. “I’m freezing, Cad.”

A large, firbolg hand is on his forehead in an instant, and Molly can’t help but relish the nice touch. He suddenly wants very much to cuddle Cad, or anything that would involve more contact that his chills are making him crave.

Caduceus makes a negative humming noise and kneels down next to the bed. “I think you might have a fever... did you feel sick before?”

Molly turns his head into his pillow and groans. He didn’t want to have to admit it, but it’s not like he has much choice. Who wants to lie to Caduceus Clay? Who would successfully lie to Caduceus Clay?

“Yeesss...” he draws out. The mattress dips under the weight as Caduceus sits on the edge of the bed and sets a hand on Molly’s back. “My whole body just seems to abhor me today.”

Caduceus hums again as he brushes curls of purple hair out of Molly’s face. Molly’s so desperate for more of the gentle touch that he sort of wants to cry. “How many times have you thrown up?”

Never mind.

Molly glares. “Wha- how did you know?”

Caduceus shrugs as if he isn’t the perceptive genius that everyone knows he is. “You haven’t eaten much today, you had to bow out on the way here.”

A huff. “Only a couple of times. Stomach’s a bit sensitive.”

Caduceus nods. “Well, good thing I have a tea for that, huh?” He goes to stand up, but Molly manages to get grip on his arm before he can. It’s not a particularly strong grip, but it gets Caduceus’ attention enough to sit back down.

“Do you mind staying? Just for a bit? I could... use a cuddle,” he admits sheepishly.

Caduceus gives one of his adorable, lazy smiles. “Yeah,” he says. “I can do that.”

Molly feels a surge of excitement go through him when he pats the other side of the bed and Caduceus climbs in. The poor guy really can’t rest comfortably in most beds that they get, he’s so fucking tall, but he manages to get as settled as he can before wrapping his arms around Molly.

Molly sighs contentedly into his friend’s chest. Caduceus is a big warm body, but more importantly, incredibly cuddly. Out of everyone in their group, he minds the least.

“Thanks, Cad,” he says with a shudder.

Caduceus gives a small chuckle that vibrates in his chest. “Anytime.”
