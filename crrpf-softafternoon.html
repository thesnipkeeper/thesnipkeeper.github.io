Soft Afternoon

Taliesin woke to a weight next to him, and then a cool hand on his forehead. He made a confused hum, although it sounded less like a hum with his congestion clogging his sinuses.

His vision was foggy with sleep, but he could see enough to catch as Matt made a guilty face. Fingers carded through his hair, slow and gentle as Taliesin fully woke.

"Hey-" He cleared his throat; it didn't help. He still sounded awful. "Hey."

"Sorry," Matt said. "Didn't mean to wake you. Was just trying to check your temperature."

Taliesin smiled sleepily and reached up to take the hand from his hair and hold Matt's hand in his own. Matt didn't bat an eye, only pressing a soft kiss to his knuckles.

"Mm, 'at time is'it?"

"Iiit's…" Matt leaned over to look at the bedside clock. "One-thirty!"

Taliesin's eyes went wide. "What?!" He moved to sit up and quickly regretted it as his vision began to swim.

"Whoa whoa, easy…"

"You're supposed to be at the studio!" Yelling at Matt was difficult when his voice was half gone.

Matt shook his head. "Took the day off."

Taliesin sighed, and then coughed. "Matt…" he said with guilty scolding.

"Nope, don't try. I wasn't comfortable leaving you alone, no one else was either."

Taliesin groaned, and let his head fall back against the pillows once again. Despite having slept for a good twelve hours, he still felt exhausted.

"Hey," Matt brushed some hair away from his forehead. "I just want to make sure that you're okay."

"I'm fine."

"I had to help you get to the couch yesterday. You're not fine. And if your fever doesn't go down I think you should call the doctor."

Taliesin scoffed. "Naw. I run pretty warm when I'm sick, that's nothing new."

Matt chewed the inside of his mouth with worry, looking him over. "Okay…"

"'m gonna go back to sleep."

Matt chuckled. "Alright, buddy. Want me to hang out in here?"

Taliesin thought, and nodded. "Please?"
