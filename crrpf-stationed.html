Title: Stationed
Fandom: Critical Role RPF
Summary: Travis makes Taliesin cry
Word count: 1.3k

Stationed

The sound of Taliesin sneezing and blowing his nose practically echoes from the other room.

"Jeez," Travis says to Matt as he walks into the kitchen.

He nods. "Taliesin's a little sniffly today." Matt takes a sip of his coffee and adds another note to the paper in front of him.

Travis scoffs, setting his coffee pod into the machine and choosing the setting that will give him the strongest coffee as fast as possible. "Sounds like he's coming down with the cold from hell."

Matt makes a sound of reluctant agreement.

"Should I... send him home?"

Matt immediately pulls his eyes from his work to look up at him. It's as if the question personally concerned him. "I... I don't know, Travis..."

"Why are you being weird? I'm just saying that we don't want the whole studio getting sick."

Matt bites his lip for a second and sighs. "Taliesin's had a bad week. I think he needs to work, honestly. Feel useful, you know?"

Travis nods slowly. He gets it. He heard a couple things about Taliesin losing a VA part one day this week, and then having to go to the vet clinic with his roommate when their cat was being put down. He hadn't thought about how multiple things could have added up for him, he hasn't talked to Taliesin one-on-one lately.

"He okay?"

Matt nods. "He's just been a little on edge. I don't think he's slept well for a couple days, and he called me last night."

Travis raises his brows, urging Matt to elaborate.

"...he sounded normal, but he kept keeping the conversation going. Asking about things that didn't really matter."

Travis knows enough about their friend to know that that's what Taliesin does when he's trying to distract himself from his own feelings. He asks everyone else about their own lives. He talks to people, keeps talking to people, until he forgets what he's worried about.

"So he's... Having a bad week." Travis hums discontentedly.

Taliesin is one if his favorite people. Definitely the most interesting. He hates the thought that his friend is suffering both mentally and physically.

Speak of the devil, Taliesin walks in and starts making tea. He scrunches his nose and sniffles, and that's the first time either of them notice the flush in his cheeks.

"Hey, do you need meds or cough drops or anything?"

Taliesin looks a bit alarmed like he's been found out. "No, no I'm fine. Snf! It's just a runny nose "

It's not just a runny nose.

Taliesin starts sneezing and coughing. His body interrupts his work more and more and he sounds so miserable Travis just wants to give him a hug forever. He's sniffling steadily, has claimed the bathroom's tissue box as his own as he wipes and blows and sniffs. It's like an unending battle with his runny nose that he'll never be able to win.

He looks exhausted. He looks like he should be at home, in bed, being fed soup and tea.

Travis sighs. "Tal, hey," he feels even more guilty for what he's about to do when he watches Taliesin slather hand sanitizer over his hands.

"Hm?"

"Do you wanna go home? You don't sound too good."

Taliesin clears his throat. "I'm fine. I don't mind- oh." Taliesin gives him a strange look. "You want me to go home." He chuckles and then sniffles.

Fuuuuck.

"Look," Can he make this better at all? "It's great that you're so ready to help out and stuff, but-"

"But you don't want anyone else to get sick," Taliesin finishes with a nod. "Right. Guess I'll see you...later, then! I'll get my stuff together."

"Feel better, Tal."

"I'll certainly try!"

Well, that was relatively painless, Travis thinks. He took that far better than he expected, especially considering what Matt said earlier.

And Travis goes about his work. For some reason, he finds himself in a strange mood. As if there's something he needs to do, but there isn't. He knows everything he has to get done. He can't imagine why he feels so weird.

Until he sees Matt coming directly for him.

Matt Mercer isn't exactly an intimidating person. He's the most forgiving person on the planet. Travis has yet to see him genuinely furious, but in this moment, he thinks that the look on Mercer's face is as close as he's ever going to get. It's not anger even, but a parent-like disappointment, which is so much worse.

"What's going on?" Travis asks as Matt lays a hand on his back and moves in closer.

The look on his face quickly changes from disappointment to... Deep concern. And now Travis just has a fearful pit in his stomach, because something must be very wrong. He follows Matt to the lounge/kitchen where Taliesin is sitting on the couch with his head in his hands. Travis can very clearly see tears running down his face, and his breathing is quick and erratic. Dani's sitting next to him, but doesn't seem to know what to do outside of offer some comforting words.

He's having a panic attack.

"Shit," Travis mutters.

Matt looks at him pleadingly. "He won't talk to us, he won't let us touch him..." He says quietly. "And I think he's starting to get a fever, which might be part of it."

Taliesin sobs and starts coughing in addition to it.

Dani bites her lip. "Do you want some water? I'll get you some water," she stands up, and Taliesin seems to react to her words just a little.

He looks up for the first time, making eye contact with Travis, and gives a self-deprecating chuckle through the tears. "I'm sorry..."

"Tal..." Travis carefully takes Dani's place next to him and sighs. "I think you need to lie down, buddy."

Taliesin shakes his head  breath still shallow and quick.

"Hey..." He risks touching him, setting an arm on his shoulder. Matt's right, he's warm. Much to Travis' surprise, Taliesin leans into the touch, just a little...

He accepts the bottle of water Dani hands him, but only holds it in his lap instead of drinking.

"I wasn't trying to kick you out," Travis tries, but that seems to be the wrong thing to say by Taliesin's quickening breath and the nervous look on Matt's face.

He isn't sure how he manages it. He's not used to dealing with anyone like this let alone Taliesin.

But Taliesin ends up fully leaned against him. Matt sits in the other side of him, and Dani's gone to help the crew with something.

"Okay, here's what we're gonna do..." Travis sighs out. He has an arm wrapped around Taliesin, who's still tense and crying. "I'm not super comfortable leaving you alone right now," to Travis' relief, Matt nods in agreement. Good, he's on the right track, then. "I'm gonna station you in here, Tal. You can still get work done, just not the same work as usual."

Matt grins.

Taliesin pulls away from him and hides his face. He's calmed down, thank God, but he's still teary eyes and sniffly. Travis isn't sure which is from the crying and which is from his cold, but either way Taliesin must be feeling all kinds of awful right now.

Travis looks up, giving Matt a pleading look. They've been friends for so long, he knows how to care for him, right?

"This is so embarrassing," Taliesin mutters.

Matt shakes his head. "Don't be embarrassed." He sits on the other side of Taliesin and presses a soft kiss to his temple. "You're really warm, though," he lays a hand on Taliesin's forehead, and of course Taliesin leans into the touch. "I have an idea."

-----

"I'm so fucking bad at this," Travis laments.

Matt shakes his head. "It's not your fault. He's just having a bad day."

Taliesin is indeed stationed in the lounge with a box of tissues and simple paperwork. Matt ends up joining him after he's done helping the others figure some things out. They're laughing and probably getting nothing done, but no one can be bothered to care after the day they've had.

And Travis finds himself joining both of them.

So much for not exposing anyone to Taliesin's cold, because they're having fun talking to him making him feel better.
