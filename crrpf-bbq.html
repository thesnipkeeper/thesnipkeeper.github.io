BBQ

They'd been planning the picnic for months. Well, "planning" was one word for it. Trying to plan. Everyone had busy schedules and it took forever to get them to like up.

And then came the magical Saturday that they were all free.

Mercer's house, 7pm, bring something to share. Travis is barbequing.

Taliesin was the first to show up. He wanted to help them set up and get ready, but if he was being honest, he wanted to spend some time with them. It's been a busy few weeks, and Taliesin missed his friends.

He used to go over there all the time, sometimes have movie nights, or sometimes he'd just show up when he needed a break. The two of them would let him sit on their couch and ignore his responsibilities..

Taliesin had felt a little more tired than usual that morning. It was a true testament to how much he needed a break.

"You didn’t have to come help!" Matt said as he let him in. "I mean, not that we don’t appreciate it."

-----

Matt sent them out on a last-minute grocery run while he got everything else set up. He knew the two of them would be more than happy to be partners in crime.

Taliesin was beginning to regret it though. He leaned on the handle of the cart and yawned. Marisha looked up from reading labels.

"You sure you're ready for a party?" She chuckled as she tossed bags of hot dog buns in the cart.

"Oh yeah," Taliesin breathed out through another yawn. His hand belatedly went to cover his mouth. "I'll liven up when everyone comes."

Marisha smiled and patted his back. "Let's go get some stuff for cocktails."

"Cocktails?"

"Laura's request. She found some cute recipes online"

-----

Taliesin went to help them bring chairs outside when Matt nudged him away.

"We've got it!"

"But there's more chairs."

"Naw, we're fine. Go wait for everyone to get here."

"Wha- they won't be here for like two hours." He was starting to catch onto Matt.

And yet, he let himself be sent to the couch. He slept perfectly fine the night before, Taliesin wasn’t sure what made him want to nap so badly. He realized just as he was falling asleep that this was Matt's plan all along.

-----

He woke to hushed voices and various sounds from the kitchen. Taliesin groaned, forcing his eyes open to see Matt and Marisha weaving around each other in the kitchen as they prepared some last-minute snacks.

Marisha was the first to look up. "Heyyyy, good morning."

"How'd you sleep, buddy?"

A blanket he didn’t remember covering up with slipped off of Taliesin's shoulders as he sat up. He yawned, rubbed his eyes, and winced at the post-nap headache that was beginning to form.

"I kind of feel like shit," he admitted. "Why'd you let me nap? You tricked me!" he pointed an accusatory finger in their direction.

Matt smiled apologetically while Marisha's own was shit-eating. "You really needed some rest…" He defended.

Taliesin ran a tired hand down his face and sighed. "What time is it?"

"Party starts in a half hour. You good?" Marisha asked.

"Yeah… yeah just a little sore." His muscles were crying out, proving that he was getting too old to be sleeping anywhere but his own bed. His body ached from having slept, and he was struggling to get used to the cold after apparently being covered up. He shuddered as the cold air from the AC seemed to target him.

He went out to greet everyone and help them bring their dishes in, while Matt and Marisha finished up the food they probably should have had prepared a while ago, but who actually did that anyway.

-----

"Taliesin!" Travis yelled from across the yard. "How many hot dogs do you want?"

"I'm good!"

"You're good?!"

"Not hungry right now!"

"What the fuck!"

Taliesin chuckled and shrugged. He was still suffering from his nap sickness. Although, he wondered how the nap could have kept him shivery outside in the LA air. His stomach had yet to tell him he was hungry despite the fact that he usually ate at this time.

Laura turned to him with an expression of confusion. She lowered her voice much quieter than her husband's "You're not hungry?"

"I'll eat later!"

There was a flash of worry across her face that confused him. He couldn’t skip a meal without people getting overly worried?

He was too tired to have decent conversation with anyone, but he found himself rambling anyway. He wasn’t sure people were really paying attention anymore, having gotten into their own conversations and jokes.

Taliesin hadn't realized he spaced off until he was nudged with a paper plate. He looked down to find a hamburger with potato salad piled next to it.

Travis smiled. "You've gotta eat. Matt said you haven't eaten since you got here."

Taliesin accepted it with reluctance. Nothing on the plate looked good right now. "I said I wasn’t hungry."

"Okay, you don’t have to eat. But you should."

Taliesin took a few bites of potato salad, but he didn’t touch the hamburger. He couldn’t. it was too much. He set the plate down and went back to staring off.

Only seconds went by before something unsettling started to happen in his gut. He was sweating, his mouth filling with saliva. He wasn’t sure what was happening, but one thing Taliesin was sure of was that he didn’t feel good. Something was wrong.

Maybe Matt and Marisha had something inside that could help.

Taliesin stood up.

Big mistake.

The world spun. He grabbed onto the back of his chair and immediately puked on the lawn.

Conversations stopped, he was vaguely aware of people asking if he was okay (as well as getting up and moving away from him to avoid getting puked on). To his horror, he wasn’t done. He heaved again, bringing up a second wave.

There went the potato salad.

"Oh God," everyone was staring. "I'm sorry… I'm- fuck, I'm sorry." humiliating. He wasn’t going to be able to look any of them in the eyes after this.

He felt a hand on his shoulder, soft and hesitant.

"Are you okay?" Matt asked quietly.

Taliesin shook his head. He wiped his mouth on the back of his hand, still panting and now wanting to cry from sheer humiliation. "I don’t- I don’t know what-" His stomach cramped, and he snaked an arm around his middle protectively.

"…'s okay." he was only half paying attention to what Matt was saying. "Hey, Tal. It's okay. C'mon, let's get you cleaned up."

Cleaned-? Oh God. There was a streak of vomit down the side of his shirt. Taliesin whimpered, trying to ignore everyone else.

"Was it the food?" Travis asked.

"I feel fine," someone said.

"I don’t think he's been feeling good today," Marisha said.

"Fuck, do you think it's-"

Taliesin lost the last part of the question but he had a general idea what Laura was asking as Matt led him into the house. He felt so drained, so sick, and achy and unbelievably hot.

Matt led him to his and Marisha's bedroom, and then their bathroom, setting him on the edge of the bathtub.

"Wanna take off your jacket? I'll wash it."

Taliesin took it off without a word. Matt walked off with it before the sound of the washing machine in the other room started. He returned with a concerned look that only Matt ever had.

"Think you're going to throw up again?"

Swallowing hard, Taliesin nodded. He was absolutely going to puke again, and soon.

"I'll give you some privacy and let everyone know you're not dying. You freaked us out a little," Matt gave an awkward chuckle.

"Sorry…" he sighed. "I didn’t even…"

"Buddy, it's alright," Matt's voice was shaking in a way that told him it wasn’t alright. He scared Matt for no reason.

And possibly brought something contagious into their home. Taliesin couldn’t help the choked sob that came out as he felt the guilt.

"Tal?! Tal, what's-?" Matt rushed toward him and sat next to him. He seemed hesitant to touch him. It just fueled Taliesin's guilt further, because Matt probably didn’t want to catch this either. Yet, his hand started stroking his back in gentle circles.

Taliesin was actually crying now. Full-on nasty crying at his best friend's party. Matt just shushed him and cuddled him. He was hot and clammy and the contact made it worse, but Taliesin allowed it because it was the only thing keeping him somewhat grounded in reality.

At some point the sobbing broke off into heaving, and he was bringing up more of what little he'd eaten earlier.

"Do you want me to go?" Matt asked when he started throwing up.

Taliesin sniffled, spitting into the toilet and shaking his head. Matt nodded.

"I'll be right here. Just take it easy," said the low, soothing voice Taliesin loved so much.

The puking eventually tapered off into dry-heaves, and then into panting as he tried to catch his breath.

"You can lie down in our room when you’re done, okay? I doubt you want to keep partying with everyone."

"Not really," Taliesin admitted before taking a wad of toilet paper and scrubbing the gunk from his mouth and nose. "Matt… I'm so sorry," he said miserably. "I can't be in your bed though, I'm probably contagious."

"Well, first of all, I don’t think you purposefully puked allover our lawn," Matt chuckled. "And we've been with you all day, so I think we're already doomed."

Fuck, he was right. "Oh God. I'm sorry."

Matt nudged him a bit with his head, an affection Taliesin realized he really needed right now to tell him everything was at least a little alright. "You really didn’t know you were coming down with something, huh?"

Taliesin shook his head, sat up and sighed. "I was just really… tired."

"That explains why you crashed on our couch in the middle of the day."

"Thought it was just 'cause I've been so busy…"

Matt hummed thoughtfully. "That's probably why you got sick in the first place."

"Yeah…"

Matt stayed with him until he was ready to lie down. He grabbed the bathroom's trash bin and set it next to the bed. The mattress dipped as he lowered himself onto the edge.

"Are you going to be okay if I go back outside with everyone for a while?"

Taliesin nodded. " 'm probably just gonna sleep."

Matt smiled, the worry still not leaving his face, and leaned in to press a kiss to the top of his head. "We'll be in in a bit. I think everyone's probably had enough partying today."

Taliesin's eyes slipped shut. "I didn’t mean to ruin anything."

"You didn’t. Get some rest, buddy."

-----

Taliesin's stomach woke him once during his nap. He choked up a mouthful of bile and fell back asleep.

He woke once more to cool fingers on his temple.

Marisha smiled softly as he opened his eyes. "Hey, how you feeling?"

Before Taliesin answered, he saw Matt taking the bin to clean it out. He made a noise and went to stop him, but Marisha's gentle touch nudged him back to the pillows.

"You're fine here, babe. Matt, can you grab the thermometer when you come out?"

"Everyone leave?"

"Laura and Travis are helping clean up still. Dishes and stuff. We just wanted to check on you for a sec."

Matt came out with the cleaned bin and thermometer a minute later.

Taliesin accepted it with some reluctance and slipped it under his tongue.

The sat in relative silence until the beep. Taliesin pulled it out with a sigh, and the reading told him exactly why he felt so awful. He hated fevers.

"101.2," Taliesin groaned.

"Well fuck," Marisha said.

Matt frowned and went back to the bathroom. "We've got fever reducers somewhere... Hold on..."

"I don't- I don't know if they'll stay down, Matt."

Matt came out with a bottle and set it on the nightstand. "I'll put it here just in case."

Marisha and Taliesin exchanged looks of fondness at Matt's nervous worthy. She grabbed his arm and pulled him down to sit down, and seemed to begin calming him just as he had been doing to Taliesin earlier.

Eventually Marisha moved so she was sitting cross-legged. Taliesin wasted no time snuggling her, resting his head on her lap and letting her sooth the misery away as she carded her fingers through his hair. He felt ten kinds of awful, but this was all that mattered right now.

Matt had gone out to help Laura and Travis. The two of them were just silent, listening to everyone chat outside the door. Marisha was probably tired as well. She was quiet enough.

"Knock knock?" The door opened, Laura peeking in. She smiled. "Heeey. Aw, you guys are cute."

Marisha chuckled. "I know, right?"

"How you feeling?"

"Uh..." Taliesin sighed. "Bad." He wanted to apologize for bringing this thing with him, but words were a lot of work.

Laura frowned in sympathy. "I um... I was thinking about how you're sick and stuff."

"Mm. Many have."

"I think Travis and I need to say sorry."

"For what?" Marisha and Taliesin said in unison.

Laura looked hesitant. "Okay, I didn't think about it until later, but Ronin was really sick a few days ago and I think we might've been carriers."

Taliesin couldn't help but laugh. All this worrying about being contagious and it turned out he wasn't the first to be blamed.

"So, sorry." She looked at Marisha. "I hope you and Matt don't- Marisha?"

Marisha's hand left Taliesin's hair, and looking up, he saw that it was now clamped tightly over her mouth. He knew that looked.

Taliesin acted as fast as he could in grabbing the bin and getting it in her lap before she expelled her dinner.

"Fuck." Laura and Taliesin said in unison.
