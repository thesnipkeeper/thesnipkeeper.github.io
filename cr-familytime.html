Fandom: Critical Role
Summary: Little Jester keeps her mom company when she's not feeling well
Word count: 300

Family Time

Jester stood in the doorway of her mother’s room with her favorite toy in-hand, fidgeting worriedly. She was going to sneak out and go adventuring today, but it didn’t sound like much fun when mama was sick.

A bunch of people were in her mother’s room earlier that morning, asking her all sorts of questions and fetching her water and medicine. Jester pretended to be asleep until the last person left her mom’s room. She wondered how early it was; the sun was shining in differently than when Jester was usually awake.

Marion was asleep now, underneath more blankets than usual and breath not quite as steady as it usually was. Maybe that was why she went to sleep so early the night before, Jester realized. Mama must have been sick then too.

Jester hugged her stuffed unicorn and took a few steps closer to her mom’s bed. The nightstand had a cup of tea on it, along with a bottle of medicine the same color of the stuff mama made her take when she was sick.

When Jester was sick, mama would lay with her and sleep in her bed every night until she felt better. 

So, the little blue tiefling decidedly climbed into her mother’s bed and got under the covers. 

“Well hello, little sapphire.” Marion’s eyes drifted open as a tired smile stretched across her lips. It was alarming how different her voice sounded from the usual smooth tone. “You shouldn’t be in here, little one. I don’t want you to get sick.”

“Don’t make me leave, mama,” Jester whined. “You’ll get bored without me.”

Marion chuckled. “That is true. Come here, let me hug you.”

Jester let herself be wrapped in her mother’s warm embrace. It was nice until she pulled away suddenly.

“Mama...!” Jester complained, until she saw that her mother was reaching for a kerchief. She gave a quick sneeze into it and wiped her nose. Oh. “Bless you.”

“Thank you, little one.”
