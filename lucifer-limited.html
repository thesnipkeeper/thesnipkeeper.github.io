Title: Limited
Fandom: Lucifer
Summary: Lucifer feels a little useless
Word count: 400

Limited

He's been sniffling all day.

Neither of them have acknowledged it, they had other things to worry about, but it's pretty clear they he's coming down with a doozy of a cold. He's had a steady flow of coffee going through his system to soothe a sore throat, giving little ineffectual coughs, and then worse ones when he was forced to run to chase the subject. He's looked wiped out ever since.

Chloe glances over to the passenger seat.

He's asleep, almost-but-not-quite snoring. Now that the case is over... He looks terrible. There's liquid at the base of his nostrils threatening to drip out, his lips parted open, and his cheeks are red.

Against her better judgement, Chloe presses the backs of her fingers to his face and feels the burning heat of a fever. He snorts awake, and then coughs wetly as he tries to regain his bearings.

"How you feeling?" There's no "getting a cold?". She knows. He knows. She's more worried about the state she's going to find him in tomorrow if he continues to pretend it's not happening and decides to spend the night drinking instead.

"Awful," he mutters, and his voice has taken a scratchy tone as if he hasn't been chugging coffee all day to avoid it.

"Go on, get some rest."

He nods and sits up, dragging a hand down his face and sniffling. He probably needs a tissue, and she doesn't keep any in the car. "Apologies for my uselessness today, Detective." Chloe recognizes the napkin that Lucifer pulls out of his pocket to be from the restaurant their suspect was working at, where they went to interview his coworkers. It looks well-used at this point as he presses it against his nose.

Chloe shakes her head with a bit of a laugh. "You tackled a killer. I wouldn't call that useless."

He huffs out something that sounds like a sigh but might just be the result of trying to breathe through his nose. He probably needs to blow it, but Chloe doesn't expect him to do so in front of her. She knows better than that.

"Still, I felt... limited..." A sniffle, and then he turns to sneeze, half into his sleeve, half into the air. Lucifer moans as he paws at his nose, scrunching it up and rubbing like it's committed a horrible sin. He finishes with a strong sniffle.

Chloe squeezes his shoulder. "Go take a nap. You did good, Lucifer."

"Trust me, Detective, that's the plan."

She smiles as he gets out of the car and sneezes again in the process.
