Do It Yourself

Patrick woke that morning to David sneezing and blowing his way through a multitude of tissues, and that was when he knew that his boyfriend’s cold had reached its peak.

It was going to be a fun day.

“Ugh, oh mby God,” David groaned after blowing his nose for the tenth time that morning. He was having some significant problems finishing his skin routine when his nose was running, and Patrick felt a twinge of sympathy as he watched David sniffle his way through applying his under eye cream.

“I don’t think I have any cold medicine,” Patrick reported sadly before pressing a kiss to David’s cheek.

“snf! It’s whatever, I cand suffer. snf!”

-----

David had been sniffling and blowing his nose all day. There seemed to be a no end to the snot he was producing, and he even told Patrick that with an annoyed groan.

He was fine to be at work outside of the fact that most of their customers would probably be coming down with colds in the next day or two. He wasn’t acting particularly rundown or tired, but Patrick knew that his nose was irritating him.

In the past two days David’s nostrils had taken on an increasingly-red tone that told just about everyone how contagious he was. It looked painful, if Patrick was being honest, and he caught David’s occasional winces when he pawed at his nose.

Patrick raised an eyebrow as his boyfriend made a quick retreat to the back of the store without an explanation, but it made sense when he heard a handful of sneezes seconds later, followed by a wet noseblow. David emerged, one hand covering his nose while the other reached across Patrick to grab the tissues from the counter, and then retreated back in to finish blowing his nose.

His nose was somehow a brighter shade of red when he finally came out, smelling strongly of hand sanitizer.

Patrick didn’t say a word, only giving him a sympathetic look. He wished he could do more for David when he was feeling so sniffly. There wasn’t a whole lot one could do for a runny nose except let it run its course and hope tomorrow was better.

Something he could help with, though...

Patrick had an idea.

He waited until the store was completely empty before turning to David, who was in the process of wiping his nose yet again and abusing the peeling red skin underneath.

Patrick laid a hand on his back and David leaned into it, though still sniffling. David did, however, tense a little bit when he realized Patrick was scrutinizing him.

“What?” he asked with a combination of confusion and annoyance, throwing his hands open questioningly, tissue still balled up in one of them.

"Your nose looks really tender,” Patrick pointed out.

David crossed his arms and raised a hand to cover said appendage. “I’m aware, thank you very much for pointing that out.”

Patrick chuckled, if not a bit sympathetically because David was obviously feeling self-conscious about it. “No, I mean...” Patrick cleared his throat. “You know, that new lotion we got in...”

David raised an eyebrow. “What are you saying?”

Patrick got off of the stool behind the counter and instructed David to sit. He went to the back to grab the canister of lotion they’d used for testing and brought it out. David was already sniffling and pawing at his nose again, this time using his sleeve, which was enough for Patrick to wince as well.

“Okay,” Patrick uncapped the jar and got closer until he and David were touching. “Stay still.”

David looked reluctant. “snf! You know I can do this myself, right?” He went to drag the back of his hand under his nose before Patrick grasped his wrist.

“Stop touching your nose, David. You’re going to make it worse.”

“Ugh! It itches.”

“Just sit still. Look up?”

To Patrick’s surprise, David did so without further argument.

There was liquid on the cusp of his nostrils, and Patrick took a tissue to gently dab it away.

“If adyode walks in...”

“It’ll be fine.” Patrick dipped a finger into the lotion, using one hand to cup the back of David’s head while he brought the other to touch his nose.

David gasped, Patrick paused. And then continued. He massaged the lotion onto David’s nose, as well as his upper lip where the redness was quickly spreading.

“Oh that’s... that’s very nice,” David admitted as Patrick brought his thumb up across his nostrils to get the last of the lotion rubbed in.

“Mhmmm. Knew it would be.”

Patrick kissed the tip of it when he was done, and David looked rather relieved.

“Thank you,” he sighed out, and then cleared his throat. “Would you um, mind doing that again later?”

“Oh, you can’t do this yourself?” Patrick asked with a smirk.

“Okay, well I’ve now learned that it’s so much nice when you do it, though. So...”

Patrick chuckled. “Yes, I can do that for you later, David.”
