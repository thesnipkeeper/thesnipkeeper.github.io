The Big Deal

"Oh my God."

Patrick rushed to where David had been getting ready in the bathroom. "David? What's wrong? Did you see a bug?"

"Fuck no," David breathed out.

When Patrick peeked in, he saw that David hadn't gotten dressed from the shower yet. Instead, he was staring at his reflection with a look of distaste.

"Why didn't you tell me?" he demanded.

Patrick leaned against the door way as David started gathering his clothes and clumsily slipping them on. He couldn’t tell why his husband was suddenly acting anxious, the way he did when something went horribly wrong and he was scrambling to fix it.

"Tell you what, David?"

"That I'm fat."

Patrick blinked. "You're not fat."

"Um," David wildly gestured at himself, pinching a bit of pudge on his belly in the process. "What do you call this?"

Patrick sighed heavily. He was never going to mention it, lest David lock himself in the coat closet and never come out again, but... “Okay, I think... we’ve both gained a little bit of weight the past few months. It’s not a big deal.”

David growled, bringing both hands up to cover his face, seemingly unsure of what else to do with himself.

“David...” Patrick approached slowly and wrapped his arms around his husband’s waist. David still smelled like his vanilla body wash and thirty-dollar shampoo. He tensed at first, but began to relax the more Patrick leaned into him. “You’re beautiful. I love the belly.”

“Oh my God,” David huffed out a chuckle. Well, a year ago a conversation like this would have had him locking himself in a room, but... things were different now, Patrick was still learning that.

Patrick pressed a kiss to his neck and chuckled. “I love you so much, and I love your body. And I know you love mine too, so...”

“Oh God.”

Patrick hadn’t acknowledged the moments in bed where David would put a little too much of his attention on Patrick’s midsection. It was certainly bordering ‘enough to be a fetish’ territory. Well, he hadn’t acknowledged it until now.

“Uh huh,” Patrick giggled. “You thought you’d get away with that, huh?”

David bit his lip and pulled back to look him in the eyes. He was trying not to smile at first, but seemed to give up on that pretty quickly. “I love you too, and yes fine, I love your belly, okay?! I think it’s cute!” he threw his arms out, passionately gesticulating before covering his face again.

Patrick hummed. “I think it’s safe to say that we’re both a little in love with each other’s bodies no matter what.”

David sighed heavily and nodded. “I think so too. So um, if you like it...”

“I love it, David.”
