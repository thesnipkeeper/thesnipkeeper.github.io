Title: Quiet Nights
Fandom: Schitt's Creek
Summary: David's falling asleep, Patrick convinces him to go to bed
Word count: 600

Quiet Nights

Patrick loves quiet nights like this. Nights after they get home from work and can just relax and bask in their married life. Even if tonight isn't quite as quiet as the others, Patrick thinks.

Patrick runs his fingers through his husband's hair, David practically melting under the touch.

David's dangerously close to falling asleep; Patrick wonders if he should bully him to bed, but he can't bring himself to. They'll deal with it later, they'll just enjoy this for right now.

His attention goes to his husband's nose when he hears  a round of sniffles.

"Tissue?"

"snf!- ugh. Yes please," David replies, sounding so tired of his body.

He's been fighting a cold all day, and if history has told him anything, Patrick knows that he'll been down with it himself within a day or two. Until then, he's just going to enjoy how impossibly cuddly David is when he doesn't feel good.

David sits up for a moment to blow his nose, making an annoyed sound afterward and flopping back down.

His nose is taking on a tender pink hue that Patrick wouldn't dare point out lest his husband quarantine himself out of embarrassment.

A few more minutes go by before Patrick can hear soft, snuffly snores.

"David, hey," he says, rubbing David's arm.

"hm?"

"Let's go upstairs. You're falling asleep."

David whines and sits up to rub his eyes and nose. He looks at Patrick rather adorably.

"It's like eight."

"Mhmmm," Patrick hummed in an admittedly patronizing tone. "Nothing wrong with going to bed a little early."

Another whine, but the look on David's face says that he knows he's right. Still, he takes a moment to think about it before rolling his eyes and nodding. "Okay, fine. I'm tired."

Patrick chuckles. "Wouldn't have guessed."

David's brows pinch together a bit, but he doesn't seem to have the energy to come up with a snarky response. Instead, he settles for pushing at Patrick and going, "Shhh..." he groans, eyes closing for a moment as he rubs his temple. Headache, then. "Um," he swallows, coughs. "I have an idea, though. Why don't you go upstairs and I'll sleep down here tonight."

Patrick blinks. "...why?"

"Um, because I'm sick? And I'm contagious." He throws out a hand and makes an 'obviously' expression. It fades as he says, "And um, I don't know how much sleep I'm going to get tonight, I don't want to keep you up. At least one of us has to be functioning tomorrow."

"David, come to bed."

David doesn't have a chance to reply before he muffles a cough into the sleeve of his sweater. As he's recovering, Patrick takes the opportunity to press a kiss to his cheek. He feels a little warm, probably has a bit of a fever coming on. Patrick stifles a sigh.

"Let's go," he says, softly nudging at David.

David rolls his eyes and makes an attempt to be dramatic as he follows.

"I hate this," David laments the moment he's in bed. He looks like a sad puppy, face smushed against his pillow while Patrick gets his pajamas on. "This is what I get for working with the masses. Why must I be punished for delivering quality products to the public?"

Patrick chuckles. "I'll make us some tea, maybe we'll both sleep a little easier tonight."

David only whines pitifully in response, but the whine is cut short as Patrick leans down to kiss his forehead.

“What did I do to deserve someone as beautiful and amazing as you?” He mumbles.

“Oh, something horrible, I’m sure.” Patrick smiles fondly, watching as his husband’s eyes slip shut. There’s a glint of moisture at the base of his nostrils, his nose is starting to run again. He’s still the most beautiful person Patrick’s ever seen.
