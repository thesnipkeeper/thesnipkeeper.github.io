Fandom: Schitt's Creek
Summary: David doesn't want to let a little earache stop them from enjoying themselves
Word count: 500

Ruined Vacation

David didn’t think he’d be whining like a kid this early in the morning, and yet... “ughh, Patrick, I’m fine.” He groans, sitting on the edge of the bed while Patrick is looking down at him with pure worry.

Patrick hums thoughtfully, uncertain, and David tries not to melt as his boyfriend lays a hand on his forehead. “I don’t know, David. You’re really warm. Are you sure you’re not coming down with something?”

“Yes, I’m sure. Totally, completely sure.” He isn’t. He’s definitely got a fever, and he’s... he’s in pain.

But this is their vacation. Their first one together and he isn’t about to blow it because his body decided to fuck him over. So what if he’s a little lightheaded, and so what if he’ll spend the whole day popping pain relievers? He and Patrick have a whole day planned!

And Patrick is still looking at him with utter doubt.

“Okay,” David sighs. “It’s just a little bit of an earache. But that’s it!” It’s really nothing to worry about. If he had the flu or something is one thing, but it’s nothing that’s contagious or obvious, so he doesn’t feel like he’s too hideous to go out in public.

But he’s starting to think that admitting that wasn’t the best idea, because Patrick’s frown grows deeper.

“What?”

“I-I mean-! It’s nothing.”

“An ear infection isn’t nothing, David. We might have to find an urgent care.”

“Oh my God, so, obviously that didn’t help my case...” David rolls his eyes. “Okay, I’m supposed to be the over-reactant one in this relationship.”

Patrick takes a deep breath like he does when he’s trying desperately not to say something rash. Except, the more David watches, the more he realizes that this isn’t frustration. It’s... distress? Disappointment?

“Look, our plans aren’t ruined,” he tries. “I absolutely refuse to fuck up this trip because I’m a little sick. I mean, my ear hurts a ridiculous amount right now, but once I get some ibuprofen in me...”

Patrick shakes his head. “David...” suddenly he’s sitting next to him and cupping his face so softly that David resists closing his eyes and falling asleep right then and there. “I’m not upset about the plans. Most of those were your idea, anyway.”

Well, he has a point.

“I’m just... I didn’t want you to spend our first official vacation together sick.”

David grins. “Well, I can assure you that I’m still capable of doing things. I might have chills and one specific part of my body might be hurting a fuck ton but that doesn’t mean that I’m about to be bedridden all day.”

Patrick smiles sweetly. His hand changes position on David’s cheek and ultimately ends up bumping his ear.

David pulls away and hisses in pain.

“Ow, fuck!”

“Oh my God, sorry!”

“No you’re-” His face is still pinched in pain, but he waves a hand dismissively in Patrick’s general direction while cupping his ear with the other. “You’re fine. I’m just going to- that ibuprofen...” he starts to get up, but a hand on his shoulder pushes him back down.

“I’ll get it.”

“Mm. Thank you.”
