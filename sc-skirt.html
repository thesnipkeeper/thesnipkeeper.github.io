Fandom: Schitt's Creek
Summary: Patrick learns something new about himself and his boyfriend
Word count: 300

Skirt

Everything seemed normal, but as David made his way around the counter, Patrick realized something was different.

"Are you wearing a skirt?"

David immediately tensed. "Uck, so I um... I haven't done laundry in a while? And all my pants are dirty, at least the ones worth wearing. And I realized, I haven't worn this thing in a while and it looks really good with this sweater, so..." He gestured to himself, and then winced. "Do you hate it?"

Patrick shrugged. "You look good. I just can't believe you found one long enough for you."

David scoffed. "Trust me, it was quite the ride. The saleswoman had fun with me, though. She didn't get to dress a lot of men."

Patrick chuckled. He loved this side of David. The "don't care what anyone thinks" side. Of course he did care, at least about some things, but he wore what he liked and said what he wanted to say. Patrick admired that.

-----

The second time Patrick saw David in a skirt was when he visited him at the motel. He'd been out with a nasty cold for the past couple of days and warned Patrick to stay away. ("one of us needs to be healthy to run the store") but he couldn’t help but drop off some soup from the café on his way home.

When he knocked on the door, he was greeted by David in a skirt once again. This one was long, it looked soft and comfortable, Patrick had to admit.

David sniffled. "Um, hi," he said, biting back a smile. "I thought I told you not to come over."

"Yeah, but I came over anyway," he held up the container as a peace offering.

David hesitated before taking the soup. "You know I can't let you come in, right? Everything is germs in here right now."

A chuckle. "Understood." He smiled, sparing another glance at David's outfit. "You look good."

"You don't have to say that just because I'm wearing a skirt, Patrick."

"No, I mean it." It was causing some… confusing feelings, and David must have noticed Patrick's blush even before he did.

"Okay," David said with a knowing smile. "So, we're going to revisit this budding kink when I'm not long leaking snot."

Patrick cleared his throat. "Sounds good."
