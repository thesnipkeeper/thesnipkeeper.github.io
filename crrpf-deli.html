Title: Deli
Fandom: Critical Role RPF
Summary: Matt almost kills Taliesin
Word count: 1.5k

Deli

There are a number of things to get done throughout the office before their D&D session starts, and he's rather proud of himself for being so productive today.

"Hey, have you had dinner yet?" Matt asks in passing.

A good question. Taliesin stops what he's doing, face screwed up in thought. Has he eaten? He certainly doesn’t remember eating dinner. Or lunch. Or breakfast. He did stop for Starbucks before heading to the studio, though, so that's something, right? At his lack of answer, Matt is now looking at him curiously, eyebrow raised.

"Okay… so I'm gonna take that as a no."

"Yeah, I mean yeah, that’s a no. I've been busy."

Concern flashes on Matt's face as he nods. "Well, I was just gonna suggest that deli down the street. That place Marisha and I have been going to."

"Ah," Taliesin says with a knowing nod. "The sketchy place that looks like a front for a meth lab."

Matt places a hand on his chest, hissing in faux pain. "Oof, watch it! That's part of the charm. It's really good, trust me."

Taliesin shrugs. His stomach growls as if begging him to consume something that doesn't have caffeine in it. "I'm starving, so why not."

"Nice! Let me know what you think."

Taliesin greets the rough-looking man behind the counter and orders a rather simple sandwich, grabbing a bag of chips for good measure. He thanks the guy, who just grunts out a "come back again".

He eats the whole thing in record time, realizing how hungry he was. Taliesin doesn't think of himself who forgets to eat often, but he was so motivated today! But oh well, now he's fed and he'll spend the rest of the night playing d&d with his friends. A good day.

-----

Taliesin bounces his knee. He swallows hard, trying to ignore the growing lump in his stomach as Matt describes their newest surroundings in the game. He's fully aware of the annoyed glances Laura is sending his way. She hates when he bounces his leg, but she's accepted that he can't exactly stop it.

He's ready for the break. A bottle of pepto was calling his name, along with some ibuprofen for the budding headache. Taliesin presses his fist to his mouth and stifles a burp, successfully managing to keep it from getting picked up on the mic.

"Scuse me," he mumbles quickly. Ashley offers a quick glance and then proceeds to ask Matt's character about shop prices.

A half hour goes by. Taliesin is no longer paying attention. He's only focused on his achy stomach. The tiniest bit of nausea is beginning to rise up, but it isn't anything he hasn't dealt with before. Another half hour and he can medicate himself to oblivion.

Another half hour, two hours into the game, and Taliesin is shifting uncomfortably, unable to sit still in his seat.

Ashley raises an eyebrow. She leans in, and he leans in as well. "You okay?"

Taliesin pulls back. He opens his mouth to answer, but honestly he doesn't have any reassuring words. He sure doesn't feel okay. Instead, he responds to Ashley's concern with a shrug.

She smiles sympathetically.

Finally, "And that's where we'll take our break! Be back in a few minutes."

Taliesin breathes out a sigh of relief and waits anxiously as the giveaway ends and the camera's turned off. He stands up before everyone else and has to brace himself against his chair. His legs are jelly, and his stomach aches so much he can hardly stand straight. Thankfully, no one pays him any mind. He gives another nauseous belch into his fist as he makes his way to the lobby.

His backpack is camped by the sofas, and he digs through it until he finds a bottle of painkillers. The others are standing around, talking and joking, grabbing snacks to tide them over for the rest of the night.

Amidst the chaos, Taliesin swallows the pills with hopes that at least his headache will go away. Now that he's seated on the couch, he isn't sure that he wants to get up again. He'll just lie back for a second while everyone is getting situated.

"Taliesin, hey," someone's nudging his arm.

Taliesin groans, lifts his head and sees Matt standing above him.  Where'd everyone else go? Fuck, how long has he been asleep?

A chuckle. "Good morning, buddy. Everyone's waiting for you, didn't realize you fell asleep," he says with a warm smile.

"Sorry, sorry..." Taliesin mumbles, running a tired hand down his face. He desperately wants to go back to sleep but they've got at least another hour and a half of game left before they call it a night.

Matt nods, smiles and makes his way to the Critical Role set to let Taliesin gather his bearings.

The short nap had done nothing but make him feel worse, Taliesin realizes. Despite how warm his skin feels, he shivers as if California is the arctic. He has to dig in his bag for a long time before he finds a rather wrinkled jacket to combat his chills, and he returns to the set.

-----

Taliesin presses a hand to his stomach not long after he returns to the game. The nausea isn't just a little nagging feeling anymore but rather a promise that he's going to throw up sometime very soon. It's making unhappy noises that he hopes only he can hear. He eventually sheds his jacket, suddenly sweating an awful lot.

Just a little while longer, he tells himself, and then he can go home and lie down, sleep this off…

One minute later and he realizes he can't wait.

Saliva starts flooding his mouth, stomach now making constant unhappy noises. It aches, and roils, and he's forced to get up and leave off-camera. Matt stutters during his description of a tavern they've just walked in as he watches Taliesin leave so quickly, but thankfully no one says anything.

He's rushing into the restroom before he knows what's happening. Taliesin stands above the toilet, panting and wincing at the pain in his stomach. He stands there for a minute before he gives up and sits on the floor, folding his arms over his cramping stomach. The nausea is as present as ever.

After a few minutes of waiting, he barely manages to scramble over the toilet when his stomach finally decides to relinquish its contents. Taliesin coughs, heaves until the bout is over. He burps unproductively and uses some toilet paper to wipe the remnants of vomit off his chin and blow his nose. He sits back and groans.

Of course he'd get sick during a weekly stream. Of all times, it had to have been the day he sits in front of a camera for four hours. Fantastic.

Everyone glances at him as he returns to his chair. He feels gross and sweaty and he really hopes his breath isn't horrible. He's shaking, he realizes, and his stomach is making awful noises as it tries to settle after what it's just been through. The nausea is ever present, but he makes it almost to the end.

Almost.

"And that's where we'll end tonight's episode! Don't forget to..."

It comes back in full force, rising in his throat and causing him to swallow convulsively. Taliesin lets out an involuntary whimper that gets the attention of Laura and Ashley, before he darts off to the bathroom once again.

Soon, he's left panting over the toilet staring sickeningly at his stomach contents.

Knock knock

Taliesin jumps.

"Tal?"

Matt. Taliesin chuckles despite the situation, because of course it's Matt. He's always been the carer of the group.

"...yeah?"

"Are you- you okay?"

Taliesin sighs and flushes the toilet. No, he's really not. He wants to cry he feels so awful. The ache in his stomach remains, and his head hurts and he's shivering again even though he knows how warm the studio is. Taliesin winces at his reflection as he washes his hands. He looks exhausted and so fucking pale.

He opens the door and ends up face-to-face with Matt, who's brows are knitted in concern.

"Food poisoning," Taliesin sighs out, before making his way to the couches. A muttered fuck can be heard behind him as the DM follows.

The others are hanging around in the lobby. Travis and Laura on their way out the door, Sam and Liam chatting idly.

"Shit, Taliesin..." Matthew looks like a guilty puppy as he watches Taliesin ease himself onto their sofa. "Was it lunch?"

Taliesin wants to say that it might not have been, but he can't. He shrugs. "It was the only thing I ate today..."

Matt's brow creases even more with worry. "Sorry."

Taliesin chuckles. "It's not your fault. I should've known better than to let you sway me!"

"What are we apologizing to Taliesin for?" Marisha chimes in.

Matt clears his throat. "I poisoned him."

"You did not poison me, Matt. Jesus Christ."

Marisha raises an eyebrow and scrutinizes him. "Yeahhh, come to think of it, you haven't been looking too good all night."

Taliesin is about to say... Something reassuring, but then his stomach decides it needs his attention once again. There's a cramp, lower this time, and he swears under his breath before leaving for the bathroom once again.

"I'm never taking your food suggestions again!" He calls back.

'That's fair!"
