Title: Siblings
Fandom: Schitt's Creek
Summary: Alexis is sick, David worries too much
Word count: 200

Siblings

"David," Alexis croaked. "Do I have a fever?"

David looked up from his book to find his sister staring at him expectantly. "I don't know."

"Okay, but can you just like come here?"

With a heavy sigh, David put his book away and went to sit on the edge of his sister's bed where she'd spent most of the day. He rolled his eyes and less-than-gently placed his palm on her forehead.

“Oh my God!” he exclaimed, pulling back.

“What?! What’s wrong?!”

“You’re warm!” David brushed his hand against his shirt. “You’re really warm! I thought you said it was just a cold!” He said, waving his hands wildly.

“It is just a cold, David!”

“Do you feel achy?”

“...Yes?”

“Chills? Loss of appetite?” Each word was said with just as much intensity as the last, and he shook the bed with every crazy gesture his hands did to emphasize.

“I... yeah?” She said. “I’m sick, of course I-”

“That’s the flu, Alexis! This,” he waved his hand in her direction. “Is the flu. You have the flu!”

“Stop saying ‘flu’, David! Why does it matter?” Alexis grumbled, nudging David off of her blanket so she could more efficiently cocoon herself.

David huffed “People die from the flu, Alexis, that’s why it matters! You should have said something!”

Alexis’ eyes were already slipping shut. “Awww,” she mumbled into her pillow. “Are you worried ab’t me?”

A sigh. “That’s irrelevant.”

Though she looked asleep, he could see a small smirk on her lips.

“I’m going out. To- to get some things. We need an actual thermometer. It’s like we’re living in the dark ages here.”

Alexis grins. “’kay.”
