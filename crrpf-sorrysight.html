Fandom: Critical Role RPF
Summary: Marisha takes care of her boys
Word count: 800

Sorry Sight

“You two are a sad, sad sight,” Marisha says with a giggle as she walks into the bedroom. In front of her are Taliesin and Matt in equal states of miserable, cuddling up together under a pile of blankets.

They each sit up as she hands them cups of tea, at Taliesin’s request.

Taliesin sniffles, wipes his nose, and sighs. “Thank you.” His nose is red from abusing it with his sleeve, but Marisha doesn’t say anything.

“Ugh,” Matt groans, echoing Taliesin’s sniffle. “Is it almost time to take more medicine?”

“Nope. Not for another two hours.”

“It didn’t even-”

“I told you that cold and flu medicine is shit and you didn’t listen to me,” Marisha sing-songs as she sits on the edge of the bed, watching her two boys inhale the steam from their tea. She smiles fondly and pulls a piece of Matt’s hair behind his ear. His hair is a mess, and so is Taliesin’s, for that matter, but it’s adorable either way.

Matt leans into the touch for a moment. He’s warm, and there’s no reason to take his temp because they’ve both been feverish most of the day.

He sighs. “I need to get up at some point.”

“What? Why?” Marisha scoffs.

Taliesin looks at him with wide eyes.

“Guys, I have to work,” he says regrettably.

“Do you really think you should be doing stuff right now?” Marisha asks.

“Well, no,” he admits. “But I’ve got to get stuff done.”

Taliesin frowns and leans over to nuzzle Matt a little bit before going back to his tea.

Matt finishes his tea and starts getting out of the bed on shaky legs. He stumbles for a moment, prompting panic from both of them.

“Matt-!” Taliesin rasps, while Marisha swears and grabs onto his arm.

He looks embarrassed, like he didn’t expect himself to be this weak. It makes sense, he’s hardly been out of bed all day.

“Okay,” she sighs. “Where we going, honey?”

“Thanks, uh, dining table.”

“Gotcha.” She kisses his cheek and clasps her other hand around his arm as well, terrified that he’s going to fall if she lets go.

Taliesin watches helplessly as they make their way out of the room.

She gets Matt settled at the table with hi work computer, and goes back to grab a blanket to keep him a little more comfortable. Taliesin looks as if he was waiting for her to return.

“Is he okay?”

“He’s fine,” she says, because he is. At least as fine as her husband can be while having the flu. “How are you holding up?”

Taliesin coughs. “Tired.”

She chuckles. “Get some rest, okay?”

He nods, and sinks into the covers. “I think that’s a good idea.”

-----

Marisha is sitting across from her husband, doing her own work as he slowly does his. It gives her peace of mind to be around in case he needs anything, but she can also tell that he just wants someone around by the way he keeps making small talk.

He sneezes and groans, doubling over for a moment.

“You okay?”

Matt goes to nod before shaking his head instead. “Little nauseated.”

“Okay, I think that’s a sign to take a break and go back to bed.”

Matt nods in agreement, closing the laptop and slowly starting to get up. Marisha rushes to his side. He gives a self-deprecating chuckle, looks like he’s about to make light of the situation. Instead, he makes a weird noise and clamps a hand over his mouth with widened eyes.

Marisha winces as her husband pukes in the kitchen sink.

-----

Taliesin’s in the middle of a harsh coughing fit when the two of them make their way back to the bedroom. He looks up worriedly, clearly being able to tell that Matt looks worse than before. Matt doesn’t wait for him to finish coughing before cuddling up to him.

Taliesin clears his throat and wraps an arm around him.

“He threw up,” Marisha says.

Matt sighs. “I was just dizzy.”

“You were sitting down.”

“Jesus,” Taliesin frowns, running his fingers through Matt’s hair.

“You two get some rest, I’m gonna-” Marisha cut herself off as the room started to spin. She quickly sat down on the edge of the bed, head in her hands. She suddenly feels very not good, she realizes. Aw, shit.

“Marisha...” Taliesin says.

“You okay?”

“I don’t feel so good,” Marisha finally admits with a sigh. She can feel chills starting to creep up, and just the general aching that comes with the flu.

She lets herself get pulled into the bed. Matt’s warm body is nice to cuddle up to, she thinks. “Sorry, guys.”

They scoff.

“Get some rest,” Taliesin echoes from before.

“Love you,” Matt mumbles as he falls asleep.

Marisha smiles despite how awful this week is going to be with the three of them down for the count. Still, they’re all together. That’s pretty nice.
