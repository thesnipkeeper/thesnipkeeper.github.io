Halfway Done

Taliesin sniffles and rubs his nose with one of the many tissues he's kept by his side during the game.

He's been dealing with a cold for a couple of days now, but it hasn't been bad enough for him to stay home. He's just done his absolute best to keep it to himself. In fact, some accused him of almost going too far.

He's well-stocked with cough drops, and while he doesn't usually try to take a lot of medicine when he's sick, he is thoroughly medicated so he doesn't sound too awful during the stream.

Laura rubs his back absently as he blows his nose, giving him a sympathetic smile.

Things go a little south when his nose starts running in the middle of the stream, and won't stop. He's reusing tissues and sniffling incessantly, probably at the expense of everyone else's ears.

Laura lays a hand on his back again, but this time she leans in and whispers, "You okay?" She pulls back to look at him, far closer than he's ever expected her to be while he's sick.

"Nose is just giving me trouble," he whispers dismissively.

"Aww, sorry." She pats his shoulder and goes back to the game.

The break can't have come sooner for him. He starts getting up even before Matt signs off, desperate to give his nose a good blow and take another dose of cold medicine. He thought that yesterday was the worst of his cold as he spent the whole time sneezing and trying to soothe a sore throat, but this is starting to prove that wrong.

He makes a quick cup of tea and finds somewhere to sit and feel miserable until it's time to return to the table.

He's looking down into his tea, sniffling and inhaling the steam in some lame hope that it'll help, when he gasps at a sudden cool hand on his forehead.

Matt offers the same sympathetic smile that Laura gave him earlier, and pulls his hand back. "Laura said you felt kind of warm," he says, taking a seat next to Taliesin on the couch.

Taliesin wastes no time resting his head on his friend's shoulder with a huff and a sniffle. "I'm okay," he says, because really, he is. He feels awful, but it is just a cold and he'll live. "I'll just be eager to get home tonight."

Matt nods against him. "I'll go easy on Caduceus tonight," he says with a chuckle.

"Oh, careful. Don’t want anyone accusing you of favoritism." He immediately pulls away from his friend to sneeze, getting everyone else's attention in the process.

"Bless you!" comes the voices of various cast and crew.

Taliesin chuckles, which results in a nasally cough muffled into his hoodie sleeve. "Thank you," he mutters, before turning to Matt. "I'm good, honestly."

Matt smiles and nods. "Okay, well, you're free to go off camera if you need to take care of yourself."

"I will," Taliesin promises. He snuggles back into his friend just as they get the signal to get back on set. Oh well, they’re halfway done, and he’ll spend time with his friends in the meantime.
