Title: Talkin’
Fandom: Blake Holsey High
Summary: Marshall and Josie have a conversation
Word count: 400

Talkin’

Josie hears footsteps in the empty dining hall. She doesn’t bother to turn around at the persistent sniffles that follow. She knows who it is.

“Hey,” Marshall clears his throat, more out of necessity than to get her attention. “Mind if I sit down?” He sounds stuffed and way too sick to be out of bed.

She doesn’t really feel like answering him. She’s afraid her voice will come out a little too broken if she tries.

“Alright, well since I didn’t hear a ‘get out of here, Marshall’, I’ll take that as a ‘sure, sit down with me, Marshall.” He pulls out the chair next to her and plops down with a sniffle. “So uh,” swipes at his nose with the back of his hand. “Do you wanna talk about it?”

“No.”

“Oka-”

“My mom promised to call and then she didn't. I don't know why I'm so surprised. I can't believe I still expect stuff from her.”

Marshall sits silently for a moment. He takes in a breath like he's about to say something, but Josie realizes that he's not going to talk, just needs to sneeze. Marshall twists as far away from her before doubling over from the force.

Josie can't help but giggle. "Bless you, Marshall."

"snf! Thanks," he rubs his nose, and shakes his head as if to clear it. "Maybe your mom's just busy, you know? Parents get like that sometimes, and it doesn't mean they like, don't care about you."

Josie huffs. "She could try to show she cares."

Marshall nods admittedly before pulling up his shirt collar to give a rattling cough. He clears his throat, “Sorry your mom’s like that.”

But Josie’s worrying about other things now. “That doesn’t sound good...”

“Hm? Oh, yeah, I forgot my cough drops in the dorm room.”

She notices the wince when he swallows, like his throat hurts on top of everything else. “Maybe you should be in bed resting.”

“Eh,” Marshall says with a shrug and a sniffle. “I just wanted to check on you, make sure you were okay.”

“Well, I’m fine.”

He nods, and he’s about to walk off.

“Wait-”

Marshall twists around on his heel, promptly loses his balance, and grabs the back of a nearby chair. “Hm?”

“Could...?” Josie sighs. “Would you and Lucas mind if I hang out in your room for a while? Corinne’s going to be studying in the library until curfew and I don’t... really want to be alone.”

Marshall smiles. “Sure, Josie. But I should warn you... since I got sick, my side of the room is kind of a war zone.”

Josie nods. “Noted.”
