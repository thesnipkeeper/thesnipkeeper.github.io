Shaken Up

"Are you sure you're good? We don't have to study tonight if you don't want to."

"I'm fine," Jamie scoffed. "Well, not actually fine. But I'd rather be with you tonight than alone."

They looked tired, a little out of it. They'd spent the whole day getting through classes with a fever. Cameron was the only person they would ever admit that to, with a simple 'I think I have a fever' at lunch. They complained of nausea near the end of the school day, and that was when Cameron knew this was going to be more than a cold.

But finally school was over, and they could both go home and relax and hope Jamie didn't have the plague. Until they were interrupted by one of Jamie's newer customers, that is.

"Yo, Jamie! That stuff you sold me was shit quality!" He yelled, approaching the two of them.

"Bullshit," Jamie muttered.

"Yeah?"

Cameron was ready to fight the moment the guy grabbed their collar and shoved them against the walls of the school.

"Hey!" He shoved the guy away from Jamie, but as usual, Jamie didn't know when to let things go.

They scoffed and dusted themselves off. "Anyone who thinks my stuff is poor quality is obviously a fucking moron."

He ripped himself out of Cameron's grip and grabbed Jamie once again. "What the fuck did you just call me?"

Damn it, Jamie...

They swallowed, eyes going to the ground. Oh God, Cameron knew that look...

Jamie shifted, sick feeling now evident on their face. "I called you a fucking moron." They made sure to enunciate. Of course they did.

The guy was livid. For a moment, Cameron was scared he was just going to straight up kill Jamie.

It happened so fast.

There was a coughing sound, and a sickening splatter, and the guy yelled and recoiled.

Jamie fucking puked on the guy.

He didn't stay after that. Cameron didn't blame him, it covered most of his front, pooling at his likely very expensive shoes.

Jamie chuckled. They were out of breath, slime dripping off of their chin, but they still had the tiniest proud smile.

"Did you time that?" Cameron had. to know.

"He shook me up a lot, it wasn't hard." Jamie scrubbed their mouth with the back of their hand with a cringe of disgust. "Serves him right."

Cameron shook his head fondly. "Let's get you home before you piss anyone else off and puke on them."
